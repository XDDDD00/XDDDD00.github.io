TaoTie Mask

Taotie mask is one of the most common patterns being seen on bronze from Pre-history to Zhou dynasty. It is characterized by a frontal animal face, the central axis is the nose, with symmetrical eyes, horns, eyebrows, ears and a nose, mouth and jaws, and some cases also have limbs, claws and tail on either side.

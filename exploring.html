YOU

'ZuoBaoZunYi'

YOU were used as ritual and drinking vessels.ts belly and sides are decorated with an ornate bird-like pattern of long, curling tail feathers, which is often considered as phoenix.The four character inscription inside are "ZuoBaoZunYi", meaning "Cast this precious vessel".

Vessel

with hunting pattern

The entire vessel is decorated with relief figural designs, the neck with a bird holding a snake, the shoulders with winged gods and figures, and the belly with a blockwork of human and animal figures, the lower belly with an outline of clouds and thunder.
